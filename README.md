# botbob-test
available commands for bot bob.


-- Movement & Positioning

bot bob hold position — Bob stays exactly where he is and watches his current angle.

bot bob follow me — Bob sticks close to the player issuing the command.

bot bob rotate to A site — Bob moves quickly to the A site.

bot bob rotate to B site — Bob rotates to B site.

bot bob push mid — Bob aggressively pushes through mid.

bot bob fall back — Bob retreats to a safer position.

bot bob flank left — Bob takes a wide left route to flank.

bot bob flank right — Bob flanks on the right side.

bot bob regroup — Bob moves to regroup with the team.

bot bob take bomb — Bob picks up the bomb if it’s nearby.

bot bob drop bomb — Bob drops the bomb where he stands.

-- Combat & Utility

bot bob hold angle — Bob holds his current crosshair position, waiting for enemies.

bot bob peek slowly — Bob peeks cautiously from cover.

bot bob rush — Bob goes full speed toward the objective.

bot bob throw smoke on mid — Bob throws a smoke grenade at mid.

bot bob flash entry — Bob throws a flashbang before pushing in.

bot bob molly site — Bob throws a molotov to clear the site.

bot bob defuse — Bob starts defusing the bomb.

bot bob cover me — Bob covers your position.

bot bob check corners — Bob sweeps corners carefully.

-- Tactical / Behavior

bot bob play passive — Bob takes more cautious positions and avoids early fights.

bot bob play aggressive — Bob pushes forward and takes duels.

bot bob watch flank — Bob guards the backline against flanks.

bot bob eco mode — Bob uses cheap weapons and plays safely.

bot bob buy full — Bob purchases full gear and armor.

bot bob hold bombsite — Bob stays and defends the site he’s on.
